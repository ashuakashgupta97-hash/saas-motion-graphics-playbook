# Final QA Checklist

## Story

- does the video clearly move from pain point to solution to impact
- does the “before” state reflect reality
- is the final positioning line strong

## Visuals

- are screenshots legible
- do overlays avoid collisions
- are highlights accurate
- do scene transitions feel premium

## Audio

- is the final voiceover the correct file
- are scene lengths aligned to the spoken line
- do UI sounds help rather than distract

## Captions

- are subtitles in sync
- are final centered lines free from duplicate subtitles
- do captions fade in and out cleanly

## Public Release

- no logo in public snippets
- no company name in docs
- no internal names in screenshots
- README preview works
