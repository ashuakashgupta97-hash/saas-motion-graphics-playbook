# Contributing

Suggestions and improvements are welcome.

Please keep contributions practical, reusable, and safe to share publicly.

Guidelines:

- avoid private company, client, product, employee, or dashboard information
- use mock data and generic screenshots wherever possible
- prefer small, focused improvements over large rewrites
- keep wording professional and tool-agnostic unless a tool reference is genuinely helpful

If you contribute examples or media, make sure they do not include logos, personal data, internal interfaces, or sensitive business details.
