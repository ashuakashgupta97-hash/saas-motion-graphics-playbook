# Story And Script Prompt

```text
I want you to help me create a premium launch-style motion graphics video for a product or business transformation story.

First, build strong context from the materials I share:
- business/process background
- screenshots
- dashboards
- example documents or inputs
- rough narration points

Do not jump straight into visuals.

Then convert the context into:
1. a clear narrative arc
2. a polished voiceover script
3. the primary message, secondary message, and emotional tone

The structure should be adaptable across:
- business pain point
- product workflow
- role of AI
- user journey
- operational impact
- final positioning

Tone:
- premium
- credible
- leadership-ready
- not flashy for the sake of it

Keep the script concise, cinematic, and suitable for a 90-150 second film.
```
